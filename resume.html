Bio


I am an aspiring commercial, television, film actor, model, stand-in and background performer from Canada. I appeared in numerous commercials on TV, social and print media such as Olympus Camera, Dodge Ram, American Airlines Chase Visa Card, Clevry and Flamingo Spa covering markets in Canada, USA, Europe and in Asia. In addition to the commercial and corporate video work, I was on films including Deadpool 2, Skyscraper, Man in the High Castle, Life Sentence, the Good Doctor, Supergirl and Altered Carbon to name a few. I also worked as a stand-in for Simu Li in Kim's Convenience. I am more than happy to accommodate to the required look and style for the roles.

Professionally, I am a Product Manager and Management Consultant and an avid sportsman. I enjoyed playing hockey and soccer, skiing and snowboarding while having completed a few half and full marathons.
